# FansSecrets - Complete Platform Feature Specification

> *Where intimacy meets exclusivity. A premium creator platform designed to feel personal, addictive, and irresistibly engaging.*

---

## The Philosophy

FansSecrets is built around one core idea: **keep everyone inside**. Every feature exists to deepen the connection between creators and their fans, making it feel like a private world they never want to leave. The experience should feel like texting someone you're into — exciting, personal, and just a little dangerous.

---

## Part 1: Must-Have Core Features

These are the heartbeat of the platform. Without them, nothing else matters.

### Creator Profiles That Seduce

Every creator gets a profile that works like a private invitation. A striking header image, a carefully crafted bio, and a grid of content that hints at what's behind the paywall. The profile should make fans feel like they're standing outside a door they desperately want to open.

Profiles show subscriber counts (social proof), verification badges (trust), and a clear "Subscribe" button that pulses with possibility. Fans can see just enough — blurred previews, locked thumbnails, teaser clips — to know what they're missing.

### Subscription System

The foundation of everything. Fans pay a monthly fee to access a creator's exclusive world. Creators set their own price (from $4.99 to $49.99/month), and once subscribed, fans unlock the feed, can send DMs, and feel like insiders.

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled. Creators can offer discounts (first month 50% off, limited-time promos) to hook new subscribers. Bundle deals for 3, 6, or 12 months at a discount keep fans locked in longer.

### The Feed: Where Magic Happens

The main content stream. Creators post photos, videos, text updates, polls, and voice notes. Each post can be:
- **Free for all subscribers** — reward loyalty
- **Pay-per-view (PPV)** — locked behind an extra one-time payment
- **Tiered** — only visible to higher-tier subscribers

The feed feels infinite and personal. Fans scroll, like, comment, and tip. Creators pin their best content to the top. Everything encourages engagement and spending.

### Pay-Per-View (PPV) Content

This is where real money flows. Creators can lock individual pieces of content — a spicy photo set, an exclusive video, a personal voice message — behind a one-time unlock fee ($5 to $200+).

Fans see a blurred preview (just enough to tease) and a price tag. One tap to unlock. The content reveals itself like unwrapping a gift. PPV can be posted to the feed or sent directly via DM for that extra-personal touch.

### Direct Messaging (DMs)

The secret weapon. Private conversations between creators and fans feel intimate and exclusive. This is where relationships deepen and wallets open.

Creators can:
- Chat one-on-one with fans
- Send PPV content directly in DMs (personal and irresistible)
- Accept custom content requests
- Use quick replies and saved messages for efficiency
- Send voice notes and photos

Fans feel seen. Creators monetize attention. Everyone wins.

### Tipping

Spontaneous appreciation. Fans can tip on posts, during live streams, or just because they want to show love. Tips range from $5 to $500 per transaction.

Tip leaderboards show top supporters (optional), creating competition and encouraging bigger tips. Creators can set up tip menus — a kiss emoji for $10, a personal shoutout for $25, a custom video for $100.

### Live Streaming

Real-time connection is unmatched. Creators go live, fans watch, chat, and tip in real-time. The energy is electric.

Features include:
- Tip goals with on-screen progress bars
- Exclusive streams for subscribers only (or higher tiers)
- Tip-activated actions (creator does something special when goals hit)
- Stream replays saved for subscribers who missed it
- Private show requests (one-on-one paid streams)

### Notifications That Pull You Back

Smart, seductive notifications bring fans back constantly:
- "Your favorite creator just posted something new..."
- "You have an unread message waiting..."
- "Limited time: 50% off subscription ends tonight"
- "They went live — join now before it ends"

Push notifications, email, and in-app alerts all work together. Fans can customize what they receive, but the defaults are designed to maximize engagement.

### Payments & Payouts

Frictionless money movement:
- **Fans pay** via credit card, debit, or alternative methods
- **Creators receive** 85% of all earnings (platform takes 15%)
- **Payouts** processed in 1-5 business days via bank transfer, PayPal, Paxum, or crypto
- Clear earnings dashboard showing daily, weekly, monthly totals
- Transaction history for tax purposes

## Part 2: Nice-to-Have / Fansly-Style Upgrades

These features separate good platforms from great ones. They're what make creators choose FansSecrets over competitors.

### Multiple Subscription Tiers (Game Changer)

Instead of one subscription level, creators can offer up to 5 tiers on a single profile:

| Tier | Name | Price | Benefits |
|------|------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Curious | $4.99 | Basic feed access |
| 2 | Closer | $9.99 | + Weekly exclusive content |
| 3 | Intimate | $19.99 | + DM access + monthly video |
| 4 | Obsessed | $34.99 | + Priority replies + custom content discount |
| 5 | Devoted | $49.99 | + Everything + exclusive live shows |

This lets creators capture fans at every budget level while incentivizing upgrades. Fans see what higher tiers unlock and feel the pull to spend more.

### Free + Paid Content on Same Profile

Creators can post free preview content alongside paid exclusives. No need for separate "free" and "paid" accounts. This lets creators:
- Attract new fans with free teaser content
- Convert free followers into paying subscribers
- Use free posts as marketing for PPV

Free content is marked clearly. The mix keeps the feed interesting and the conversion funnel healthy.

### Discovery / "For You" Page

A powerful built-in discovery system so fans find new creators without leaving the platform:
- **Trending** — creators gaining subscribers fast
- **Categories** — fitness, art, music, cosplay, lifestyle, etc.
- **Recommended** — based on who they already follow
- **New Creators** — fresh faces to discover
- **Near You** — location-based (optional for creators)

The algorithm learns what fans like and serves more of it. Endless scrolling, endless discovery, endless spending.

### Mass Messaging

Creators can message all subscribers (or specific segments) at once:
- "New PPV dropping tonight — be the first to see it"
- "Going live in 30 minutes, don't miss it"
- "Special offer for my top tippers only"

Mass messages can include PPV attachments. One message, thousands of potential sales.

### Content Organization

- **Folders** — organize content into albums (e.g., "Beach Day," "Behind the Scenes")
- **Series** — sequential content that fans can binge
- **Tags** — searchable labels for easy discovery
- **Pinned posts** — highlight best content at profile top

Fans browse like a private media library. Better organization means more content consumption.

### Scheduled Posts

Creators queue content in advance. Set it, schedule it, forget it. Perfect for:
- Maintaining consistent posting while traveling
- Dropping content at optimal engagement times
- Planning promotional campaigns

### Geo-Blocking

Creators can block content or entire profiles from specific countries/regions. Essential for:
- Privacy (block your home country)
- Legal compliance (different content rules by region)
- Peace of mind

### Custom Tip Menus

Visual menus showing exactly what tips buy:
- $10 — Heart emoji reaction
- $25 — Personal thank you message
- $50 — Name mentioned in next post
- $100 — 60-second custom video
- $200 — 10-minute video call

Clear, visual, irresistible.

### Custom Emojis & Reactions

Creators upload personal emoji packs. Fans use them in comments and DMs. Builds brand identity and community.

## Part 3: Admin & Moderation Tools

Behind the scenes, keeping the platform safe and running smoothly.

### User Management Dashboard

Admins can:
- Search users by username, email, or ID
- View account details, earnings, and activity
- Suspend, ban, or warn accounts
- Reset passwords and 2FA
- Review verification status

### Content Moderation Queue

All content flagged by AI or reported by users enters a review queue:
- View flagged content with context
- Approve, remove, or escalate
- Add strikes to accounts (3 strikes = ban)
- Track moderator actions for accountability

### Automated Content Scanning

AI-powered detection for:
- Underage content (zero tolerance, instant removal + ban)
- Non-consensual content indicators
- Illegal activity
- Copyright violations

Human moderators review edge cases. Speed matters.

### Report System

Users can report:
- Content (inappropriate, illegal, stolen)
- Accounts (impersonation, underage, harassment)
- Messages (threats, spam, unsolicited content)

Reports include category selection and optional description. All reports logged and tracked.

### Analytics Dashboard (Admin)

Platform-wide metrics:
- Total users, creators, subscribers
- Daily/weekly/monthly revenue
- Content volume and type breakdown
- Payout totals and pending amounts
- Flagged content trends
- Chargeback rates

## Part 4: Compliance & Safety Features

Non-negotiable. These protect the platform, creators, and fans.

### Age & ID Verification (Creators)

Before posting any content, creators must:
1. Submit government-issued photo ID
2. Take a live selfie for facial matching
3. Confirm they are 18+ years old
4. Sign digital consent and terms agreement

Verification processed within 24-48 hours. No verification = no posting.

### Age Verification (Fans)

All users must confirm they are 18+ to access the platform. Options include:
- Credit card verification (card on file = age confirmed)
- Third-party age verification service
- ID upload (optional, for higher trust accounts)

### 2257 Record-Keeping

Full compliance with record-keeping requirements:
- All creator IDs stored securely
- Consent forms digitally signed and archived
- Records available for legal inspection if required
- Custodian of records designated

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Optional but encouraged for all users. Required for:
- Creators withdrawing earnings
- Changing payout details
- Account recovery

Options: SMS, authenticator app, email codes.

### Blocking & Muting

Users can:
- **Block** — completely hide from each other, no contact possible
- **Mute** — hide from feed without blocking
- **Restrict** — limit interactions without full block

Creators can block entire regions (geo-blocking).

### Content Consent System

For content featuring other people:
- Creators must confirm all persons are 18+ and consented
- Digital consent forms for co-performers
- Model release agreements stored on file
- Regular audits of multi-person content

### Abuse Reporting & Response

Clear reporting flow:
1. User submits report with details
2. Report enters priority queue
3. Moderators review within 24 hours
4. Action taken (warning, removal, ban)
5. Reporter notified of outcome

Emergency escalation for severe cases (threats, illegal content).

### Privacy Controls

Users control:
- Who can message them (everyone, subscribers only, no one)
- Profile visibility (public, subscribers only, hidden from search)
- Activity status (show/hide online status)
- Read receipts (on/off)

## User Flows

### Creator Journey

**Sign Up → Setup → Earn**

1. **Create Account** — Email, password, agree to terms
2. **Verify Identity** — Upload ID, take selfie, sign consent forms
3. **Build Profile** — Add bio, profile photo, header image, set username
4. **Set Pricing** — Choose subscription tiers and prices
5. **Post Content** — Upload first photos/videos, set as free or paid
6. **Promote** — Share profile link on social media
7. **Engage** — Respond to DMs, go live, post consistently
8. **Get Paid** — Watch earnings grow, request payouts

### Fan Journey

**Discover → Subscribe → Engage → Spend**

1. **Browse** — Explore discovery page, search categories, see previews
2. **Find Someone** — Click into a creator profile, see blurred content
3. **Subscribe** — Choose tier, enter payment, unlock the world
4. **Consume** — Scroll feed, watch videos, read posts
5. **Engage** — Like, comment, tip on posts
6. **Go Deeper** — Unlock PPV content for extra-special moments
7. **Connect** — Send DMs, request custom content
8. **Stay Hooked** — Notifications pull them back, subscription renews

## Part 5: Building This in 2026

### Option 1: No-Code / Low-Code Tools

**Tools:** Bubble, Softr, Glide + payment integrations

**Pros:**
- Fastest to launch (4-8 weeks for basic MVP)
- Lowest upfront cost ($500-$5,000)
- No technical team needed initially

**Cons:**
- Limited customization
- Scalability issues at high volume
- May hit feature walls quickly
- Payment processor restrictions on adult content

**Best for:** Testing the concept before investing heavily

---

### Option 2: Clone Script / White-Label Solution

**Options:** xFans, Suspended, Fanso, or similar OnlyFans clone scripts

**Pros:**
- Pre-built features (80% done)
- Faster than custom (8-16 weeks to launch)
- Moderate cost ($5,000-$30,000 for license + setup)
- Some customization possible

**Cons:**
- Shared codebase with competitors
- Limited unique features
- Dependent on script vendor for updates
- May have hidden costs and licensing fees

**Best for:** Launching quickly with proven features, then customizing

---

### Option 3: Custom Development

**Stack suggestion:** Next.js + Supabase + Stripe Connect + Mux (video) + Cloudflare (CDN)

**Pros:**
- Full control over features and design
- Unique competitive advantages possible
- Scales properly from day one
- Own all code and IP

**Cons:**
- Longest timeline (4-8 months for full MVP)
- Highest cost ($50,000-$200,000+)
- Requires skilled development team
- Ongoing maintenance costs

**Best for:** Serious operators building for long-term success

---

### Recommended Approach for 2026

**Hybrid strategy:**

1. **Start with a clone script** — Get to market fast, validate demand
2. **Customize heavily** — Add unique features that differentiate
3. **Migrate to custom** — Once revenue justifies it, rebuild properly

Timeline: 3 months to MVP, 6 months to feature parity, 12 months to custom platform

Budget range: $15,000-$50,000 for initial launch, $100,000+ for full custom rebuild
